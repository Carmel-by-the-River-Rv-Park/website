Our Park

Family owned and operated for over 40 years, our boutique park is nestled into the lush natural surroundings of Carmel Valley and has all the amenities you need to enjoy your stay on the Monterey Peninsula.

Click to Enlarge

We offer full-hookup sites (including water, sewer, electric, wifi and cable tv) with the option of 30 or 50 amp. Your site also comes outfitted with a picnic table and charcoal grill for your enjoyment so you can sit back and relax in our serene setting as the soundtrack of chirping frogs lulls you to sleep every night.

Guests are welcome to enjoy our recreation room with a lounge area, air hockey, billiards, and ping pong tables. For your convenience there is a general store located in our office, which offers non-perishable groceries, cold soft drinks, and RV supplies. Clean bathrooms and hot showers are also available for your comfort. The Carmel River is a natural area and is not accessible from the park. River access is available nearby at Garland Ranch Regional Park, a short drive away.

Frequently Asked Questions

Please view our FAQ to answer any common questions you may have. For any other questions, please call our office at +1 (831) 624-9329.

How big is your park?

We are a small-boutique park with only 35 sites total. The layout of the park is in a loop which is just over an eighth of a mile, so the whole property is about half the size of a football field. It is a garden-like setting, with lots of foliage, flowers, and trees throughout the park. We are tucked away in a residential area, so it is quiet and peaceful.


Do you have sites on the river?

The river runs along the base of the property line, so unfortunately there is no direct view, or waterfront, and there is nothing recreational allowed since it has not been accessible for over 10 years since the drought. The closest access to water is Carmel Beach, located off of Scenic Road in Downtown Carmel-by-the-Sea which is 8 miles West from our location (10 minute drive - not in walking distance from our park). The town is very small and has narrow roads, so there is no RV parking available.


Are your sites pull-through sites or back-in sites?

Most of our sites are back-in sites. We do have a handful of pull-forward sites for motorhomes only, but they are not pull-throughs as you have to reverse when leaving.


Do you have full hookups?

Yes we do. We offer sites with either 30 or 50 amp.


Are there hiking trails from the park?

Our park is surrounded by private property and there are no hiking trails from the park. Closest hiking trails are Garland Ranch Regional Park (5.2 miles East of our location; dog friendly), and Point Lobos (8.6 miles Southwest of our location; not dog friendly)


Are there fire pits at the sites?

Fire pits are not provided however you may burn charcoal or Duraflame logs in your site’s grill. Both are available at the Park Office. Propane fire pits are also available for rent. We do have a seating and dining area outside the Park Office with a propane fire pit for guest use.


Can I pick my site when making reservations?

Final site assignments are made the day of your arrival. We will take requests and try our best to honor those requests.

Do you have a swimming pool?

We do not.


Do you have Uber/Lyft in the area?

There are taxis, Uber, Lyft, and car rental agencies in our area that you could look into as transportation.


Do you have a laundry room?

We do not. The closest laundromat is 3 miles down the road at the MidValley Shopping Center


Do you have a dumping station?

We do not have a dumping station. If you would like to dump then you have to rent a site at the daily rate to use the hookups.


Communication Response Rate

Our office is inundated with all communications - email, calls, and voicemails - and we are responding to them as quickly as possible in the order that they were received. We only have one phone line that is extremely busy, so if you get our voicemail you can leave a message and someone in our office will call you back as soon as they can. If you do not leave a message we will not know to call you back. You can also send your information via email to secure your reservation. All of our reservations are first-come-first-serve basis as we are a small boutique park and only have 35 sites total.


Confusion on Arrival & Departure Dates

Our confirmation is written as your arrival date to your departure date, just like a hotel reservation.


How long does it take for a refund to process?

Our terminal closes the batch the night your refund was processed, pushing your refund through our system the following day. Please allow ample time for your bank's processing. With most financial institutions it can take 7-10 business days before you see a reflection on your account.
