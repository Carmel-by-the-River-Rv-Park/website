Privacy Policy

Carmel by the River RV Park values your patronage and respects the importance of your privacy.

This Privacy Policy ("Policy") describes the information collection, use, protection, and sharing practices of Carmel by the River RV Park QJV website.

By using, browsing, or accessing the Carmel by the River RV Park site (collectively, “Carmel by the River RV Park website or company’s property website addresses”), you agree to the terms of this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree to the Privacy Policy, please immediately log off the site and do not use it.

Information We Collect

We collect information from a variety of sources and in a variety of ways, including without limitation, the following:

Computer IP Address

We collect your computer’s Internet protocol (IP) address, which you necessarily disclose when you visit the Carmel by the River RV Park QJV website so that our computer may send data (such as the web pages you request) to your computer. The site utilizes anonymous “cookies”, unique identifiers that web pages on the site may use to store data on your computer or send data to our web server. We may use cookie data to “recognize” you when you visit the site in the future, to troubleshoot how the site operates, and for internal analysis of how people use the site. Should you decide not to accept cookies from the site, you will limit the functionality we can provide when you visit the site.

During your stay at one of our properties, if you choose to log onto our website via the use of the resort provided Wi-Fi, we might collect information about the websites you visit, the type of device and browser you use, your device identification number, your geographic location, session time, etc.

Personal Information

By entering your personal information on our site or giving it to us in any other way, such as voluntarily giving us your information by email and conducting business with us on this site or in person you are choosing to share your personal information with us. You will see that the amount and type of personal information required by us depends on the activity you are engaging in. For example: to reserve a campsite, you must provide personal information (such as name, address, and telephone number) and financial information (such as credit card number and expiration date). We use this information to bill you or to fulfill your reservation or other purchase. If we have trouble processing your reservation or other purchase, we will use this information to contact you. When you voluntarily provide us with your personal information, we may use the personal information you have provided for certain activities, including, but not limited to:

  • Processing your reservation
  • Processing your credit card payment
  • Collections of payments

We respect the privacy of your information and we take precautions to protect the information you share with us. We will comply with our credit card merchant bank’s requirements for the use and storage of your credit card information such as obligations concerning the security of your credit card numbers as well as any laws that apply to the use and storage of that information. Although we cannot guarantee against loss, misuse, alteration, unauthorized disclosure or destruction of data, we use commercially reasonable security measures to protect you from such occurrences. You acknowledge that: (1) there are security and privacy limitations in computer systems and on the Internet which are beyond our control; (2) the security, integrity, and privacy of any and all information and data exchanged between you and us through the Site cannot be guaranteed; and (3) any such information and data may be viewed or tampered with by a third party while such information or data is being used, transmitted, processed, or stored.

If you have provided an email address and or personal mailing address, you have voluntarily given us permission to contact you for certain marketing purposes, including, but not limited to:

  • Notifying you of special promotions
